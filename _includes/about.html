What is Time Stamp?

Time Stamp is THE work time tracker in the Windows Phone store. It is easy to use and feature rich. After more than 4 years in development and available since Windows Phone 7 it became the best-rated work time tracker in the store. Compare for yourself and try it out. Have a closer looke at some of the features below.

Variable work times

Configure your work times in Time Stamp according to your work contract. Use variable break times and detailed daily work/break times for customization.

Time saver

Just open Time Stamp, start your work time and quit. No need to create projects or customers beforehand. Do the same to end your work day.

Comprehensive report

Use the report to show your work times on a weekly, monthly or yearly basis. You can even export the report to Excel.

NFC & GPS* support

Use either NFC or GPS support to automatically start or end your work time tracking. With GPS there is even no need to start the app at all as work times are started or ended depending on a predefined geolocation.

* GPS is available in Time Stamp v3. Join the Beta now!

Export & Backup

Export and Backup your data to various destinations including OneDrive, DropBox**, Google Drive**, E-Mail** and Local Storage**

** Supported in Time Stamp v3. Join the Beta now!

Still on-going

Time Stamp is after more than 4 years still in development. More features coming in the future :)
